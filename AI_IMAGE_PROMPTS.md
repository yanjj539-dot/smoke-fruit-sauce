# AI Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts for Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, 即梦, 可灵, Aegis AI, or similar tools. Keep all outputs original; do not copy Bucks Sauce assets, labels, copy, trademarks, or bottle design.

## Product Bottle Images

### 中文

原创高级小批量辣酱品牌 SMOKE & FRUIT 的单瓶产品图，正面居中构图，完整瓶身可见，统一瓶型，黑色瓶盖，深棕纸质标签，奶油色背景，柔和自然阴影，高级食品品牌包装质感。分别生成三种口味：Crushed Pineapple Heat 使用菠萝黄色点缀，Smoked Habanero Garlic 使用烟熏橙和辣椒红点缀，Cherry Chili Glaze 使用樱桃红点缀。不要真实品牌 Logo，不要 Bucks Sauce 相似设计，不要手，不要道具。

### English

Single premium hot sauce bottle product image for an original fictional small-batch brand named SMOKE & FRUIT. Centered front view, full bottle visible, consistent bottle shape, black cap, deep brown paper label, cream background, soft natural shadow, refined premium food packaging. Generate three flavors: Crushed Pineapple Heat with pineapple-yellow accents, Smoked Habanero Garlic with smoked-orange and chili-red accents, Cherry Chili Glaze with cherry-red accents. No real logos, no Bucks Sauce similarity, no hands, no props.

- Recommended ratio: 1:2 or 4:5
- Recommended background: cream `#F5E4C7`
- Recommended style: realistic editorial food product photography
- Negative prompt: cartoon, neon, cyberpunk, glassmorphism, copied brand, gray placeholder, distorted label, unreadable AI text, cheap ecommerce

## Floating Ingredient Images

### 中文

透明背景或奶油色背景的单个高质感食材图，用于高级食品官网 Hero 漂浮动效。分别生成番茄、红辣椒、菠萝块、大蒜、樱桃、洋葱片、香料颗粒。真实食品摄影或高级 3D 食材渲染，柔和高光，自然阴影，色彩与深棕、奶油米色、辣椒红、菠萝黄、樱桃红、烟熏橙统一。不要文字，不要卡通，不要明显 AI 痕迹。

### English

Single high-quality ingredient asset for floating Hero motion on a premium food website. Create tomato, red chili, pineapple chunk, garlic clove, cherry, onion slice, and spice granules as separate assets. Transparent or cream background, realistic food photography or high-end 3D ingredient rendering, soft highlights, natural shadow, palette aligned to deep brown, cream, chili red, pineapple yellow, cherry red, and smoked orange. No text, no cartoon, no obvious AI artifacts.

- Recommended ratio: 1:1
- Recommended background: transparent if supported, otherwise cream `#F5E4C7`
- Recommended style: realistic premium food photography
- Negative prompt: cartoon, emoji, neon, plastic, messy background, text, watermark, cheap stock image

For 6 Pack, show six bottles grouped with depth. Cream background, warm natural light, soft shadows, small garlic, chili, and pineapple garnish. Packaging matches the single bottles: black caps, deep brown labels, red/yellow/orange flavor accents. No real trademarks, no sale stickers.

- Recommended ratio: 3:2 or 16:10
- Recommended background: cream `#F5E4C7`
- Recommended style: premium ecommerce product photography
- Negative prompt: cartoon, neon, discount badge, real brand logo, distorted bottles, unreadable labels
